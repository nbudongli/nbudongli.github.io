Information

  1. Dr. Li Dong is an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor in the Department of Computer Science at the School of Information Science and Engineering, Ningbo University, where he also serves as the Deputy Director. He earned his Bachelor's degree from Chongqing University in June 2012. After graduating, he received Master's degree from the University of Macau in December 2014, and his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Macau in September 2018. In November 2018, he was appointed to Ningbo University as part of the "Outstanding Ph.D. D1" talent introduction program.
    Dr. Dong has received numerous academic honors, including full graduate scholarships from the University of Macau (2012-2014) and a full Ph.D. scholarship (2015-2018). He was named the Outstanding Graduate of the University of Macau in 2014 and received the "Teacher of Backbone Talent" award from the Ministry of Education and Huawei Smart Foundation in 2023. Dr. Dong also won the Lege Teacher Innovation Award in 2020 and was recognized as an Excellent Class Advisor at Ningbo University in both 2020 and 2022. Additionally, he received the "Advanced Individual" award at the 20th China Graduate Mathematical Modeling Competition. Under his mentorship, students achieved First Prize (TOP 1.38%) in the 17th Huawei Cup Graduate Mathematics Competition, won Best Student Paper at the SPDE2020 International Conference, and received the Huawei Ascend Gold Quality Award.
    Dr. Dong's primary research interests lie in multimedia forensics and security, as well as statistical image modeling and processing. He has published over 30 papers in international journals and conferences, including more than 10 in prestigious IEEE Transactions journals. Dr. Dong has led several research projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (Youth Fund), Zhejiang Provincial Natural Science Foundation, the Open Research Fund of the CAD\&CG National Key Laboratory at Zhejiang University, and the Ningbo Natural Science Foundation (Youth Doctor Innovation Project). He has also spearheaded industry-funded initiatives, including the Alibaba Innovation Research Program and Huawei's Ascend AI Model Development Project.
    Dr. Dong holds 12 national invention patents, four of which have been granted, and one US patent. He is also the author of five software copyrights. In addition, Dr. Dong serves as a reviewer for leading international journals, including IEEE TIP, TIFS, TCSVT, and TMM.
